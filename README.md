# Quantum Exchange Notes

Lecture notes building a conceptual and mathematical bridge from elementary algebraic ideas (commutativity, isotropy, idempotence, projection) to quantum-mechanical exchange rules for identical particles, culminating in anyons and topological quantum computing.

## Topics Covered

- Basic algebraic language (Abelian operations, isotropy, idempotence)
- Projection operators and their eigenvalues
- The exchange operator and the derivation of p² = 1
- Bosons (symmetric exchange) and fermions (antisymmetric exchange)
- Spin-statistics connection
- Why 3D restricts exchange to bosons and fermions
- Anyons in 2D: Abelian and non-Abelian
- Experimental status of anyons (fractional quantum Hall effect)
